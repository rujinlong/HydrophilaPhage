## 逐页要点

**S2 — Who I am**
- I *close the loop* myself: isolate → characterize → sequence/annotate → safety screen → in-vivo
- 落点:这让 discovery→validation 很快 → 今天给一个 aquaculture pathogen 的完整案例

**S3 — ⭐ A. hydrophila case(王牌,慢讲)**
- This is the punchline of why we're a good fit
- Novel phage vs *A. hydrophila* — **正是你邮件点名的病原** — from a diseased largemouth bass
- Single dose, 1 h post lethal challenge: survival **15% → 85%**
- Histology: liver/spleen largely protected · genome clean (no ARG / no virulence / strictly lytic)
- 收:not theoretical — already validated on an aquaculture-relevant system

**S4 — Transferable toolbox**
- 每步都是标准模块;稳定性正好覆盖 aquaculture 水体 temp/pH
- 收:give us a new pathogen — a *Vibrio* — we plug it into the same pipeline

**S5 — What we need**
- We can run the pipeline, but we don't know which pathogens matter in German aquaculture, and lack characterized fish-pathogen isolates (esp. *Vibrio*)
- 收:these gaps aren't weaknesses — they're the interface to your group

**S6 — Why your side**
- Clean fit, not overlap. You: disease + isolates + model + network. Us: discovery + omics + mechanism
- 强调 network:连接 diagnostic labs / farms 是我们无法复制的

**S7 — Two pilots**
- Pilot A 最贴你邮件:你给 1–2 个 priority isolate → 我们 isolation + host-range + genome/safety → 3–6 mo 一个 characterized phage panel
- Pilot B:迁移我们 *A. baumannii* phage–antibiotic 经验,机制+应用一次到位
- 不必 day-one 承诺复杂动物实验

**S8 — Discussion(转入对话)**
- 抛 4 个问题,然后闭嘴听:pathogen? / isolates? / validation model? / 3–6 mo outcome?
- 收:co-design one small project we can start in the coming months

## 备用 Q&A(可能被问)
